Our Story

Makers Engineering Limited was founded with a bold vision: to redefine the construction and engineering landscape in Juba and across South Sudan. Born from a desire to bridge the gap between ambitious infrastructural needs and world-class execution, we established our roots by committing to uncompromised quality and technical excellence.

From constructing roads and bridges that connect communities to erecting commercial and residential landmarks, our journey is a testament to resilience and expertise. We blend deep local knowledge with international engineering standards, ensuring every project we deliver stands the test of time and drives sustainable progress for the nation.
